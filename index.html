Why most GTM stalls
Most teams don't have a lead problem. They have an integration problem — the pieces of their go-to-market don't add up to an engine.
  • Great product, vague positioning. You can't articulate why you win, so every deal turns into a price conversation.
  • Activity without pipeline. Outreach is happening, but nothing predictable comes out the other end.
  • A sales motion that doesn't match the buyer. The way you sell doesn't fit how your market actually buys.
  • No system, just heroics. Revenue depends on one or two people instead of a repeatable engine.
